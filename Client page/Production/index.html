What does unique clicks mean?

There’s three types of “clicks.” First there’s the unique click which is a unique person who visits your website.

Next, there’s the raw click which means that one person visits your website more than once, create 1 unique click and several raw clicks.

Third, there’s fake clicks. These clicks appear as unique clicks, but they never convert to optins. We don’t do fake clicks. And you will only have unique visitors counted towards the total of your order. This way, you get more than you pay for!
